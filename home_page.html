About Us

We are a volunteer-based organization in West Malaysia that helps
the blood donation process and it's distribution to the blood banks.
Red Heart has been operating in West Malaysia since July 2015 and have
been handling the donation of blood up to 5 million blood bags with the
help of 200 thousand volunteers throughout the years.
